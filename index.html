Types of Cats

Scottish Fold

The Scottish Fold is known for its adorable folded ears, round face, and calm, sweet nature. They are affectionate, playful, and enjoy being around people.

Ragdoll

Ragdolls are gentle, blue-eyed cats that love to be held and cuddled. They are relaxed, friendly, and great for families or first-time cat owners.

Persian

The Persian cat is elegant and fluffy with a calm, gentle personality. Known for its flat face and luxurious coat, it loves quiet environments and lots of pampering.
